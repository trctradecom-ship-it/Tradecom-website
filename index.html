Why TRC Has Transaction Tax

Tradecom (TRC) applies a small transaction tax to ensure long-term stability, healthy liquidity, and protection for our community members.

The tax structure helps reduce extreme volatility, discourages short-term dumping, and strengthens liquidity through buybacks and liquidity additions.

This model supports long-term holders and promotes sustainable ecosystem growth.
